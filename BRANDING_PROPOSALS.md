## üî§ Font Recommendations

### Primary Font Options:

1. **Space Grotesk** (Recommended)
   - Modern, geometric sans-serif
   - Excellent readability
   - Professional yet approachable
   - Great for fintech applications

2. **DM Sans**
   - Clean, versatile
   - Excellent number readability (important for financial data)
   - Modern and professional

3. **Inter** (Current - Keep but enhance)
   - Already in use
   - Excellent readability
   - Add weight variations for hierarchy

4. **Poppins** (Current - Good choice)
   - Friendly yet professional
   - Good for headers

### Font Pairing Strategy:
- **Headers/Brand:** Space Grotesk or DM Sans (bold, 700-800 weight)
- **Body Text:** Inter (regular, 400 weight)
- **Numbers/Data:** DM Sans or Inter (monospace variant for tables)
